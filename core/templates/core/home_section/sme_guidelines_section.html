Are you a SME ?

In response to economic changes since 2005—like inflation, structural shifts, and evolving business trends—Nepal revised its SME definition, officially endorsed at the 14th NSDC meeting in July 2013.

Why SME Matters In Koshi Province?

Over 90%+ of businesses in Nepal are MSMEs, driving economic growth and jobs. With 923K+ establishments (2018), many are based in Koshi Province, including key hubs like Birnagar and Itahari. SMEs contribute 22% to GDP and 70%+ of non-farm employment.
